Citizenship

"the quality of an individual's response to membership in a community" (Merriam-Webster)

Honesty

Honesty is when one is sincere or truthfull. This is important to a good citizenship because each citizen has a pledge to their fellow citizens to be truthful about them and their actions.

Compassion

Compassion is when one is concious of anothers distress. This is important to a good citizenship because a good citizen must be aware of other's misfortune and sadness in order to benefit the community as a whole.

Respect

Respect plays a very important role in a good citizenship. Respect is important because one must have respect for their community, the people in their community, and the rules of the community.

Responsibility

Responsibility is very important to having a good citizenship. People must have a responsibility to maintain their community and themselves.

Courage

Courage is important to having a good citizenship because if one has courage, then the progression of the community as a whole is accelerated. It is important because people must have the courage to speak up about issues and wrongdoing.

Intelligence

Intelligence is important to a good citizenship because if one is not intelligent they cannot make educated decisions to maintain a good citizenship. One must be intelligent to be a working part of their community.

Acts of Citizenship

One of the most recent acts of Citizenship that I have seen was in my neighborhood. One of my neighbors took it upon themselves to move people's trash cans out of the street. This shows responsibility, and intelligence.
